# -INSURANCE-RISK-CLAIMS-ANALYSIS
1. Project Title / Headline
Insurance Insights: Risk & Claims Analysis Dashboard
A dynamic and interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze insurance policy distribution, claim behavior, and risk patterns across customer demographics and vehicle attributes.

 2. Short Description / Purpose
The Insurance Insights Dashboard is a comprehensive Power BI report that analyzes large-scale insurance data to uncover risk patterns, claim trends, and customer segmentation insights. It helps stakeholders make data-driven decisions in policy management, risk assessment, and claim optimization.

 3. Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

• 📊 Microsoft Power BI Desktop – Main platform for building interactive dashboards
• 📂 Power Query – Used for data cleaning, transformation, and preprocessing
• 🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Created KPIs and calculated measures
• 🧩 Data Modeling – Built relationships between policy, customer, and claims data
• 📈 Visualization Tools – KPI Cards, Bar Charts, Donut Charts, Line Charts, Tables
• 📁 File Format – .pbix for development and .png for dashboard preview

 5. Features / Highlights
 Business Problem
The insurance industry deals with massive datasets containing customer, vehicle, and claim information. However, extracting meaningful insights manually is complex and inefficient.

-Key questions include:
Which customer segments are high-risk?
What factors influence claim frequency and amount?
How do policies vary across demographics and vehicle types?
Which segments contribute most to claims?

-Goal of the Dashboard
To develop an interactive analytics tool that:

-Identifies high-risk customer segments
Analyzes claim trends and policy distribution
Enables multi-dimensional filtering and drill-down analysis
Supports data-driven decision-making in insurance operations

-Walkthrough of Key Visuals
-Key KPIs (Top Section)
Total Policies
Total Claim Amount
Average Claim Frequency
Average Claim Size

-Customer Segmentation (Bar / Donut Charts)
Distribution by age group, gender, education, marital status
Identifies high-risk demographic segments

-Vehicle Analysis (Bar Charts)
Policies by car make, car use (private/commercial), car year
Highlights vehicle-related risk patterns

-Coverage Zone Analysis (Donut Chart)
Policy distribution across urban, suburban, and rural zones

-Trend Analysis (Line Chart)
Policies over car manufacturing years
Shows growth and decline patterns

-Comparative Analysis (Tables / Charts)
Claim amount by education and marital status
Helps identify high-claim contributing segments

-Interactive Filters (Slicers)
Age group, gender
Car use, make, year
Coverage zone
Education and marital status

 -Business Impact & Insights
 Risk Identification: Detects high-risk customer groups based on demographics and behavior
 Better Decision Making: Helps insurers optimize pricing, policies, and claim handling
 Customer Segmentation: Enables targeted marketing and policy customization
 Trend Analysis: Identifies growth patterns in policies and claims
 Operational Efficiency: Reduces manual analysis through automated insights
 Data-Driven Strategy: Supports smarter business and risk management decisions
